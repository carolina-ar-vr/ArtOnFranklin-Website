How Does it Work?

1. Use your phone camera to scan the small QR code in the bottom right corner or visit artonfranklin.com manually in your web browser.

2. From the website, place your phone in front of the black and white box business logo marker to see art pop up on your screen.

3. Touch, swipe, or pinch to rotate, flip, or zoom in on the art on your phone!
